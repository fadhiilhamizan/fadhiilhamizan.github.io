Atmosfer

A weather dashboard whose sky changes to match the forecast.

Search any city (or use your location) for current conditions, a 24-hour hourly strip, and a 7-day outlook — with the whole interface re-tinting to the real weather and time of day. Built around a backend proxy that keeps the API key off the client, caches responses, and normalizes provider data behind a clean contract.

Naufragus

An underwater adventure that teaches kids to save the ocean.

A single-player Roblox adventure: after carelessly littering, you're swept to the seafloor and must clean up trash, free trapped sea creatures, solve puzzles, and parkour your way back to the surface. Designed to teach children 7–15 about Indonesia's marine biodiversity and the plastic crisis. Built with Team 54 — and awarded 1st place in Game Application Development at ISCOM 2024.
